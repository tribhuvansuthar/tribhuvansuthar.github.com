About

KNACK LETS CREATE

A feather floating finds a way to HUES and so does a person full of Knack. Those muddle heads carrying ignited souls have the fire to burn everything down only to make the best shine brightest. The spark that needs air to fire up and make a mark is hidden in the fingers running out to paint the canvas. Sparkling meteor falls down to scatter the intensity. Talent is shown as a vision of our own through the dark never ending road. We are blessed with a knack to pull wonder out of extraordinary. Our talents are living things, we give birth to them, nourish them till they grow and become immortal.

KNACK is testing our limits and skills with confidence. It is tracing a bird's flight to form a flawless path to infinity. It is an open wild space where we know no limits, where black and white is as beautiful as a rainbow, where a waterfall pours colours and its splashes touch each heart
