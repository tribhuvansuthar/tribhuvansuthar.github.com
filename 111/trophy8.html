War Of Band

"Cut it." The purpose of the theme is to not let any performance be genre specific. Each band will be given a time limit of 10 mins. The number of songs within that time duration isn't limited, cutting back to the chase, the number of songs that a band performs, half of them should be on "half-beat".
For eg. - 5 songs means that either 2 or 3 songs have to be on the " half-beat". Genre is not specific.
