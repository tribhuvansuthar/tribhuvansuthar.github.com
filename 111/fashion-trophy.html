FASHION

“FASHION is not something that exists in dresses only. Fashion is in the sky, in the street, fashion has to do with ideas, the way we live.”
Expose your talent as an architect and designer, rather than only being a clothing designer or model. Create your attires which personify the architect in you, by accessorizing your dressing like cloth, footwear, hats using building materials. Satiety ramp with your “knack” and get ready to amaze the audience by your outstanding ideas.
Think of carpet, tiles, curtains and even the back of a chair. Open up your Pandora box of creativity and add the spice of glamour with it. Who knows the monotonous materials used in your homes may become the future of the fashion industry, spellbinding one and all with your ‘knack’
