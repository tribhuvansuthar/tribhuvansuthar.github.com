MAIN DESIGN

While most of us sit at home and crib about how the world has gone haywire, there are a few, the selected few amongst us who give their all to the motherland and protect us with all their might and indomitable courage. It is because of them that we are assembled here today to participate in a big event. In fact, it is because of those brave souls that we are able to breathe without a care in the comfort of our homes. We owe a lot to our soldiers.

The Soldier is the army. No army is better than its soldiers. The soldier is also a citizen. The highest obligation and privilege of citizenship is bearing arms for one’s own country. We can hardly compare to the great deeds accomplished by these brave hearts but we as architects can surely contribute to make their dwellings better. No soldier outlives a thousand chances. But every soldier believes in chance and trusts his luck.

Therefore, we propose students to design billets keeping in mind the concept of chimera in such a way that the barracks are seen as a hocus pocus by the enemy forces, just like a mirage in the desert.
