LANDSCAPE

Trophy

LAURIE BAKER

Our ancestors fabricated a structure to commemorate a notable person or event to keep it alive for ever. These structures are losing their identity as they’ll blow away with sand of time, if we don’t perpetuate them well. To sustain, prosper the panorama is what we need to work on. Prospering will modulate the structure to a mid enthrallment.

A site is said to be developed in all aspects only when its landscape has been planned well. A site without the visual essence of landscaping elements is often rendered unattractive and thus incomplete. In order to fulfill the condition of breathing life to a structure’s environment, architects have been working diligently on the tedious task of landscaping. Keeping in mind this spirit, we propose students to design the landscape in the context of some unknown site so as to incorporate surreal beauty of landscapes into the site.

The non- prominent heritage site of your city that has lost its essence of landscape has to be re done in a way that it re gains its beauty and stands apart. Show that hidden talent, the “knack” in you to convert that barren land into something beautiful.
