FRENCH CLASS

French Class is a non-fiction romantic comedy, set in the year 2011, in an engineering college. The central character has been in love before and in unable to understand the weird things people do when they fall in love. He ridicules them, mocks them and most of all pities their state of being. All this, until, he meets a girl in his French Class. What follows is a roller coaster side of fights and make-ups and his desperate attempt to get out of the dreadful friend-zone. In the middle of all this, he also ends up promising to marry a girl he meets online. So, what would it be? The girl online or the girl in his French Class?
